## Code of Conduct

We follow the Contributor Covenant (v2.1). By participating, you are expected to uphold this code.

Standards

- Be respectful and inclusive
- Focus on constructive feedback
- Assume good intent; disagree with ideas, not people

Enforcement

- Report incidents to the maintainers as per `SECURITY.md` contact details
- Incidents will be reviewed promptly and confidentially
- Actions may include warnings, temporary or permanent restrictions

Attribution

- Based on Contributor Covenant `https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/`
