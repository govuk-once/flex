## Security Policy

Reporting a vulnerability

- Please email security reports to security@gds.gov.uk (or your internal security contact)
- Include steps to reproduce, impact assessment, and any logs
- Do not create public issues for sensitive reports

Triage SLA

- Acknowledge within 2 business days
- Initial assessment within 5 business days

Embargo and disclosure

- We may request a private disclosure window while we prepare a fix
- We will coordinate public disclosure and release notes

Severity

- We follow CVSS as a guide and prioritise actively exploited or high-impact issues
